Fact from 4-12-2019
408 parts per million.
The concentration of carbon dioxide (CO2) in our atmosphere, as of 2018, is the highest it has been in 3 million years.
Source: https://www.conservation.org/stories/11-climate-change-facts-you-need-to-know

Fact from 20-11-2019
Price tag climate a: US$ 140 billion per year.
This is what it would take to make the changes humanity needs to adapt to a warming world.
It may sound like a lot, but it’s less than 0.1% of global GDP.

Fact from 13-11-2019
Nearly 1 million hectares lost.
An area of coastal ecosystems larger than New York City is destroyed every year,
removing an important buffer from extreme weather for coastal communities and releasing carbon dioxide into the atmosphere.
Source: https://www.conservation.org/stories/11-climate-change-facts-you-need-to-know
