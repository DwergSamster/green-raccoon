The start of The Green Raccoon

In 2019 we needed to start a suitable business with economics. In a group of 4 we started this website with recycling in mind.
For the logo and name we thought about an animal that recycles. One of us thought about raccoons because they eat leftovers and use resources that they find in nature and share them with other raccoons.
